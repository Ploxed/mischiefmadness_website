Mischief Madness

Mischief Madness is a free-roam singleplayer/multiplayer action shooter! Play as Dallas in a currently small choice of arenas. Battle opposition, raid houses, and live off what you have until you can't. Rank up and become the most respected gang member ever. Can you survive the brutal life of Dallas?
